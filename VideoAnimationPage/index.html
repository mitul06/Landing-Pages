Take Only Memories,

Leave Only Footprints

This isn’t just one of those cute travel quotes, it is also a motto everyone should live by in life. We need to give back to this world. People often feel they can take home a trophy, or sneak a piece of stone from a historic site or take something from nature. It isn’t about taking, it is about absorbing the beauty of the world.
